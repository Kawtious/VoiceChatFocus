### What is this?

My group has 4 different voice channels in our server, and yet we all hop into the same channel. Long story short: it's hard to hear the person I am playing with over 6 other people all talking at the same time - all doing different things

This plugin adds a button to focus on a single user's voice by muting everyone else in the voice channel

### How do I install it in BetterDiscord?

Drop `VoiceChatFocus.plugin.js` into your `plugins` folder
